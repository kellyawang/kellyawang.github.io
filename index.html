ABOUT ME

Kelly Wang is a Senior Computer Science/Art double major at Williams College who is interested in the role of the Arts in Technology, and of Computer Science in Art. She is happiest when she has a lot to do and is learning something new. She also enjoys painting while listening to music on her headphones, baking and decorating pastries with her sisters, and dancing quite visibly in public places.
