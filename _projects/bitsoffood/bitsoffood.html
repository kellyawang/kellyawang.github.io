--- layout: details name: bitsoffood projectId: bitsoffood workTitle: Bits of Food videoLink: 'https://drive.google.com/file/d/1T33GaQk8tFvrYam0iRconUvpnCr3dKqG/view?usp=sharing' workDetails: - label: DATE data: Nov 2019 - label: MEDIA data: D3, JavaScript, HTML, CSS summary: - line: These visualizations were created as a final project for CS171 (Data Visualization) at the Harvard Extension School - line: We were interested in creating a data-driven view of Africa's food production, dimensioned by social, environmental and economic factors. Generally, global food production, population, emissions and trade values are increasing, and we compare these worldwide trends across the African continent to discern the current status and potential opportunities and concerns. To bolster this high-level overview, we examine the same factors within defined geographical regions and countries - allowing users to learn more about specific statistical features. Furthermore, we model these trends toward predictive futures in order to preview a possible outcome if the data trends continue. Our early analysis suggests that there will be significant challenges, which we intend to emphasize along with potential strengths that might serve as a basis for potential solutions. - line: The data to support this project is available through the Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations (”FOA”) data portal. http://www.fao.org/faostat/en/#data ---
